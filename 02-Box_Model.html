Understanding the Box Model

In this example, we have a box with various components:

  • Content Area: The blue area represents the content area.
  • Padding: The space between the content and the border is the padding.
  • Border: The black border surrounds the padding and content.
  • Margin: The space outside the border is the margin.

We use the box-sizing: border-box; property to include padding and border in the element's dimensions.

We have also added two additional properties to the box: border-radius and box-shadow.

  • border-radius: This property controls the roundness of the box's corners.
  • box-shadow: This property creates a shadow around the box.
